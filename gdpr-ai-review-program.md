# GDPR and AI Review Program

This file records the external review and conformance path for the GDPR and ISO AI mapping waves.

## public_law_anchor_review

- Goal: Challenge GDPR article-level and obligation-level anchors, including rights and processor boundaries.
- Required roles: `privacy_counsel`, `data_protection_engineer`, `public_interest_reviewer`

## ai_standards_candidate_review

- Goal: Challenge candidate-only ISO AI mappings and the decision to publish or withhold them.
- Required roles: `ai_standards_specialist`, `ai_assurance_reviewer`, `formal_methods_reviewer`

## claim_schema_review

- Goal: Challenge whether typed claim schemas match the intended GDPR and AI evidence-bearing atoms.
- Required roles: `compliance_engineer`, `privacy_counsel`, `ai_assurance_reviewer`

## public_docs_review

- Goal: Challenge whether the public site overclaims what is reviewed, proved, or only candidate-level.
- Required roles: `privacy_counsel`, `ai_standards_specialist`, `public_interest_reviewer`

## Review phases

### inventory_preflight

- Goal: Ensure current framework inventory, coverage, and publication boundaries are recorded before asking for external review time.
- Pass criterion: Framework priorities and coverage are current.
- Pass criterion: Candidate-only and zero-coverage states are explicit.
- Pass criterion: Publication boundaries are documented.

### public_law_review

- Goal: Challenge the widened GDPR article-level layer and its rights-related and processor-related control boundaries.
- Pass criterion: Article-level anchors are challengeable and clearly sourced.
- Pass criterion: Public wording stays honest about proved versus attested privacy controls.

### ai_candidate_review

- Goal: Challenge candidate-only ISO AI mappings before they are widened into the public control surface.
- Pass criterion: Candidate mappings are either strengthened, blocked, or retired.
- Pass criterion: Draft-watch frameworks are not presented as stable reviewed layers.

### public_release_review

- Goal: Check that the site and public repos do not overclaim the state of GDPR or AI standards mapping.
- Pass criterion: The public site keeps reviewed, candidate, and watch-list states explicit.
- Pass criterion: No page implies certification or legal sufficiency from partial mapping coverage.
